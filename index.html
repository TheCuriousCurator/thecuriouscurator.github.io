Introduction

Welcome to the ML Design Thinking & Platforms Thinking course. This 8-week program is designed to help you master the principles of design thinking and platform strategies in the context of machine learning.

Program Details

Week 1
  • Introduction to Markets
  • Appreciating the complexity of products like Google Image Ads, Uber, Netflix, Spotify.
Week 2
  • Understanding User Engagement
  • Candidate Generation
Week 3
  • Ranking & Relevance
Week 4
  • Clicks & Conversion Signals into Utility, Fixing the baised clicks and tuning the Utility.
  • Market Design 1: Auction & Pricing
Week 5
  • Market Design 2: Matching Models
  • Market Design 3: Assignment & Alloction Problem
Week 6
  • Optimizing Rankings for Recommendation in Matching Markets.
  • Market Design 4: Matching with bids.
Week 7
  • Ranking with Long-Term Constraints.
  • Multi-Objective Optimization.
Week 8
  • BUFFER. But I will make sure that there is content for this incase we are on schedule.
  • Farewell: Overview of what we covered. Discussions.
